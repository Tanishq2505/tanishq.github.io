Objective

During the Barclays' Data Steller Hackathon, my team embarked on a remarkable journey and clinched the coveted first prize for our innovative solution to the Customer Onboarding problem statement. Our winning solution involved the creation of a powerful Flutter application complemented by an intuitive Admin Panel, revolutionizing the account creation process for new users.

In our application, we implemented multiple layers of authorization to ensure a comprehensive onboarding experience. Notably, we incorporated video verification as the final step, further enhancing the security and authenticity of the process. As a key contributor to the project, I took charge of developing the captivating front-end design for the mobile application. Leveraging my expertise, I seamlessly integrated multiple APIs to facilitate seamless communication between the front-end and the backend, which was skillfully implemented with Kafka. Additionally, I employed Provider, a powerful state management solution, to ensure a fluid and dynamic user experience.

Our triumph in the hackathon not only showcased our technical prowess but also exemplified our dedication to simplifying customer onboarding and streamlining the account creation journey. TripWise stands as a testament to our commitment to excellence and innovation, and the first-place accolade reaffirms the effectiveness of our solution in the competitive landscape.
