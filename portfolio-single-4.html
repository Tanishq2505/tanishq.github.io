TripWise

Date Feb 2023
Type Internship Project
Role Full stack developer - Flutter + Flask-Python
URL Play Store

Objective

During my internship at IIT Guwahati, I had the opportunity to contribute to an exciting project called TripWise. As a Flutter-based mobile application, TripWise utilizes location-based services to empower users in planning and executing their trips with ease. This comprehensive application offers a one-stop solution for travelers, allowing them to explore travel destinations and conveniently book their itineraries within a single platform. Within the project, my focus primarily revolved around the front-end development, where I worked diligently to create an intuitive and visually appealing user interface. Additionally, I successfully integrated APIs gathered from various websites, ensuring seamless data retrieval and enhanced functionality. As a valuable member of the team, I also collaborated in hosting a Flask + Selenium application on an AWS EC2 instance, leveraging Python to deliver a robust and scalable solution. Through my contributions to TripWise, I gained invaluable experience in mobile app development, API integration, and cloud hosting, further solidifying my skills in creating impactful applications that simplify travel planning for users worldwide.
