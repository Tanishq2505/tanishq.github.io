Acadata

Date March 2021
Type Personal Project
Role Full stack developer - Flutter + Flask-Python
URL Play Store

Objective

Seeking to address the frustrations caused by our college's cumbersome student portal, I embarked on a mission to create Acadata—an innovative mobile application that brings all the crucial data from the website directly to your fingertips. By harnessing the power of Flutter as its interface and leveraging the robust combination of Selenium and Flask in Python as its foundation, Acadata offers an aesthetically pleasing application with a fluid and reliable user interface. Seamlessly integrating essential information, this transformative tool puts everything you need right in the palm of your hand. Please note that Acadata is currently discontinued.
